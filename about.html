About Me

I’m student at Cornell pursuing a major in computer science and minor in mathematics. Throughout my undergraduate career I’ve gotten the opportunity to work with deep learning, bioinformatics, natural language processing, and backend development. Looking ahead, I will graduate May 2021 and join Google as a software enginneer. I'm passionate about learning and cannot wait to both explore new areas and dive deeper into familiar ones!

Outside of work/school, I’ve been obsessed with photography since I was a kid. My parents gifted me a DSLR during my senior year of high school, which I had been begging for nearly a decade. You can see some of my work, primarily focused on natural landscapes, in my (amateur) photo blog hosted on this site. In general, I love being outside touring scenic areas, and the conservation of our environment is immensely important to me. I’ve also been playing the violin since age 7. I stopped practicing in college, but hope to get into it again afterwards!

As an Asian-American, I was born in Shanghai, China and shortly afterwards my family immigrated to the US. I then spent the rest of my childhood throughout Texas. Afterwards, I moved to upstate New York for university and will reside in the Bay Area after graduation. I am grateful for my time in each location and hold all of them close to my heart :)
