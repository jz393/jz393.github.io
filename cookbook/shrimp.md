### ingredients
- ~1 lb jumbo shrimp (around 10 pieces)
- salt
- black pepper
- paprika
- garlic powder
- olive oil

### steps
1. remove shell of shrimp and remove excess moisture, then place shrimp into bowl
2. add spices to shrimp and mix thoroughly:

    - salt, ~1/4 tsp
    - black pepper, ~1/4 tsp
    - paprika, ~1/4 tsp
    - garlic powder, ~1/4 tsp
3. heat up skillet and coat with a thin layer of olive oil
3. place shrimp on skillet and cook for ~2-3 min on each side, or when they become fully pink

### notes
- This usually serves as an additional source of extra protein, rather than a standalone meal.
- I get my [shrimp](https://www.safeway.com/shop/product-details.970062637.html) from Safeway (it's often on sale).
