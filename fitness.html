Current stats

  • Basics: 26F, 5'1, 93lbs
  • RHR: 50-60 bpm daytime avg, 43-48 bpm night
  • VO2max: 35-40
  • Running pace: ~12-13 min (staying in zone 2/3)

Notes

  • I run/walk outside at a nearby park, for around 30 min. I typically run a mile in zone 2/3 and walk another mile.
  • I do strength training at the gym, focusing mainly on arms (sometimes legs, but I don't want to overdo it to affect my runs/hikes).
  • I usually go hiking at the Dish, which takes roughly an hour. If I'm strapped for time, I'll just go to the park as usual.
  • The weekends are typically free, as I usually end up doing something outside anyway. If not, I sometimes go to the gym as well.
  • This is flexible and subject to change depending on how I'm feeling each day. For example, if my legs are sore, I'll walk instead of running.
